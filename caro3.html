LION

The lion is a wild terrestrial animal called the king of the forest. The lion is a strong animal with a strong body, a big head, a majestic mane,
and two fierce eyes. Lions are predatory animals and eat only after hunting. They have strong claws and sharp teeth,
which help them hunt their prey and eat the flesh.In mature females, the height at the shoulder is around four feet
the weight is about 450 pounds, the length is approximately eight and a half feet plus its tail. As a result, females are considerably smaller and weigh around 300 pounds on average.
